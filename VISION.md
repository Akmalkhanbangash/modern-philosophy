# Modern Philosophy

## Mission

Help people think better so they can live better.

---

## Principles

We do not build for clicks.

We build for clarity.

We do not chase motivation.

We build systems that encourage consistent action.

Every article should leave the reader with one useful idea they can apply today.

---

## Product Philosophy

A quote should not only be read.

It should stay in the reader's mind throughout the day.

An article should not only teach.

It should inspire action.

Every visitor should leave with a clearer mind than when they arrived.

Modern Philosophy is not another content website.

It is a place to slow down, reflect, and act.

---

## Experience Philosophy

Reading should feel calm.

Not distracting.

Not overwhelming.

The product should encourage deep thinking.

Silence is a feature.

Focus is a feature.

A peaceful environment is a feature.

---

## Future Experiences (Version 2+)

After Version 1 is complete, Modern Philosophy will evolve into a mindful reading experience.

Possible features include:

- 🕯 Candle Reading Mode
- 🌧 Rain ambience
- 🌊 Ocean sounds
- 🌲 Forest ambience
- 🔥 Fireplace mode
- 🎧 Listen to articles
- 📖 Distraction-free Focus Mode
- 💭 Daily reflection prompts
- 📝 Personal notes and highlights
- ❤️ Save meaningful quotes
- 📅 Daily philosophy practice

Features are added only when they genuinely improve thinking—not simply because they look impressive.
